ABOUT ME

03

Who I am?

I work with authors who want to publish with intention, not just urgency. I'm interested in design that supports a voice, not distracts from it. I don't see design as decoration, but as narrative structure. That's why every book I design is different—because every story demands to be read in its own way. I work remotely, closely. With rigor, but without solemnity. And yes, I have strong opinions about typographic quotation marks.
